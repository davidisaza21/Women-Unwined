Women unWine Wednesday

Women unWine Wednesday was started to connect the next generation of creative women in Miami. We wine, dine, and have casual conversations about creativity, brand building, business, and life. Join to cultivate new friendships, enjoy of curated wines and even more curated topics. Women unWine Wednesday, hopes to inspired collaboration by connecting talented like-minded women in Miami.

Founded by Daniela Hernandez, WUW is a monthly gathering limited to a small group of boss ladies. If you’re a female creative entrepenuer or student and would like to join, please sign up below, it’s free to join!
