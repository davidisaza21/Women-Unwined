*unWined: to unwind with friends and wine

Women unWined wants to connect the next generation of professional women in Miami. We wine, dine, and unwind about life and business or as we call it “unWined”. Join to cultivate new friendships, in joy of curated wines and even more curated topics.

Women UnWined aims to inspire collaboration between professional like-minded women. Started by Daniela Hernandez and friends at Studio Uni, the monthly gathering is limited to a small group of ladies. If you’re a female entrepreneur or student and would like to join, please check the dates below.
