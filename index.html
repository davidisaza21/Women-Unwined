*unWined: to unwind with friends and wine

Women unWined wants to connect the next generation of professional women in Miami. We wine, dine, and unwind about life and business or as we call it “unWined”. Join to cultivate new friendships, enjoy of curated wines and even more curated topics.

Women Unwined aims to inspire collaboration between professional like-minded women. Founded by Daniela Hernandez, the monthly gathering is limited to a small group of boss ladies. If you’re a female entrepreneur or student and would like to join, please RSVP below.
